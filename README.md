# Power-BI-Project---1
Blinkit Data Analysis using Power BI

 Insights from the Dashboard:

 Fat Content Contribution
·       Low Fat items generated $425K in sales.
·       Regular items performed even better with $776K, showing higher customer preference.

Sales by Item Type
·       Top categories: Fruits & Vegetables ($0.18M) and Snack Foods ($0.18M).
·       Moderate sales: Household ($0.14M) and Frozen Foods ($0.12M).
·       Lowest sales: Starchy Foods and Breakfast items (<$0.02M).

Sales by Outlet Size
·       Medium outlets dominate with $507.9K sales.
·       Small outlets contributed $444.7K.
·       High-size outlets made $248.9K.
 Insight: Medium-sized outlets bring the maximum revenue.

Sales Outlet Location
·       Tier 3 locations lead with $472.1K sales.
·       Tier 2 contributes $393.2K.
·       Tier 1 follows with $336.4K.
Insight: Rural/suburban (Tier 3) markets outperform urban (Tier 1).

Sales by Outlet Type Performance
·       Supermarket Type1 is the leader with $787.6K sales and 5,577 items.
·       Grocery Stores: $151.9K
·       Supermarket Type2 & Type3: ~$130K each.
Insight: Supermarket Type1 contributes over 65% of total sales.

Sales by Outlet Establishment Year Trend
·       Sales started at $78K in 2012 and peaked at $205K in 2018.
·       Slight decline afterward but stable around $130K–$132K in recent years.
Insight: 2018 was the strongest performing year.
